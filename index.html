Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Vibly collects and processes data for both regular Users and Business Partners (Merchants).

1. Data Collection and Account Types

A. For Regular Users

Location Data (GPS): We collect your precise or approximate location only while the app is in use to display nearby offers and deals. We do not track your location in the background.

Personal Information: To create a User account, we collect your:

  • Email Address
  • Password

B. For Business Partners (Merchants)

Location Data (GPS): We use location services to allow you to set the physical location (pin) of your business branch on the map.

Personal & Business Information: To register a Business account, we collect:

  • Email Address & Password
  • Phone Number (for contact and verification)
  • Business Location Coordinates

Verification: Business accounts are subject to verification/approval by the Vibly administration before they become active.

2. Data Deletion and Account Removal

Both Users and Business Partners can request the deletion of their accounts and associated data.

  • How to request deletion: Contact us at wiselab1992@gmail.com.
  • In-App Deletion: Go to Profile > Settings > Delete Account.
  • Data Retention: Upon request, personal data is deleted within 7 business days. Note: We may retain non-personal transaction records if required for legal or tax compliance.

3. Security

We use HTTPS/TLS encryption to protect your data during transit to our Firebase servers.
