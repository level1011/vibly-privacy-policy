Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

This document describes how Vibly collects and processes your data.

1. Data Collection and Use

Location (GPS)

Vibly collects location data to provide information about deals and offers near you. We collect and process your location data only while the app is in use (Foreground Location). We do not track your location when the app is closed.

Personal Information

For registration, we collect:

  • Full Name
  • Phone Number
  • Email (including via Google Sign-In)

2. Data Deletion and Account Removal

We provide a way for users to request the deletion of their accounts and associated data.

  • How to request deletion: You can request account deletion by contacting us at wiselab1992@gmail.com.
  • In-App Deletion: You can delete your account directly within the app via Profile > Settings > Delete Account.
  • Data Retention: Upon request, all personal information, location history, and media files will be permanently deleted from our servers (Firebase) within 7 business days.

3. Security

We use modern encryption (HTTPS/TLS) to protect your data during transit to our Firebase servers.
